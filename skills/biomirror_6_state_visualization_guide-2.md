# BioMirror — Visualization Guide for 6 Physiological States

This guide defines a visual language for six BioMirror body states. The goal is to make each state immediately readable through body overlay, aura, texture, motion, and color — without relying on text explanation.

---

## 1. Calm

### Visual feeling
Calm should feel stable, clean, slow, spacious, and breathable.

### Color palette
- Main color: soft cyan / blue
- Secondary color: turquoise / pale green-blue
- Brightness: medium-low but clear
- Contrast: low to medium

### Body appearance
- Reflection/body silhouette should look clear and stable.
- Edges are softly glowing, not sharp.
- Body surface is smooth with minimal texture noise.
- No aggressive distortion or glitch.

### Aura behavior
- Thin, even aura around the body.
- Aura slowly expands and contracts like breathing.
- Shape should remain symmetrical and stable.
- Low turbulence.

### Motion style
- Slow wave movement.
- Fluid-like transitions.
- No sudden bursts.
- Motion should feel predictable and continuous.

### Surface texture
- Smooth holographic skin.
- Gentle gradient over the body.
- Optional subtle wave lines moving from chest outward.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| Low HR / stable HR | slow chest pulse |
| Low EDA | low noise / high clarity |
| Low SCR frequency | no flash or spike effects |
| Low motion | stable aura |

### Avoid
- Red/orange colors
- Heavy glitch
- Fast flashing
- Camera shake
- Dense particles

## 2. Stressed

### Visual feeling
Stressed should feel compressed, tense, noisy, and internally activated.

### Color palette
- Main color: red-orange / hot magenta
- Secondary color: electric cyan for contrast
- Brightness: medium-high around chest/head
- Contrast: high

### Body appearance
- Body is still readable, but reflection is unstable.
- Upper body, chest, neck, and head should show more disturbance.
- Edges may vibrate or split slightly.
- The body can look like it is under pressure from inside.

### Aura behavior
- Aura contracts tightly around the body.
- Aura pulses quickly and irregularly.
- Short bursts appear near chest/head when stress spikes occur.

### Motion style
- Rapid micro-pulses.
- Short horizontal glitch movements.
- Slight jitter around face and shoulders.
- Avoid full chaotic movement; stress should feel tense, not totally broken.

### Surface texture
- Scanlines.
- Light digital noise.
- Subtle pixel displacement.
- Sharp pulse rings around chest.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| High HR | faster chest pulse |
| High EDA | stronger distortion/noise |
| High SCR frequency | frequent small glitch bursts |
| High SCR amplitude | stronger flash/ripple events |
| Low movement + high EDA | internal noise without large body movement |

### Avoid
- Fully smooth calm visuals
- Too much particle celebration
- Large open aura
- Making it look energetic/positive like Active

---

## 3. Anxious

### Visual feeling
Anxious should feel trapped, restless, tight, and mentally overloaded while the body remains relatively still.

### Color palette
- Main color: violet-blue / cold purple
- Secondary color: pale cyan
- Brightness: concentrated around head, chest, and hands
- Contrast: medium-high

### Body appearance
- Body silhouette remains mostly still.
- Head and chest show concentrated disturbance.
- The reflection should feel like it is trembling internally.
- The body may appear slightly compressed inward.

### Aura behavior
- Aura stays very close to the body.
- Aura is narrow and vibrating.
- It should not expand outward freely.
- Around the head, aura can become denser or foggier.

### Motion style
- Micro-jitter.
- Small fast vibrations.
- Swirling motion around head.
- Tight circular motion around chest.

### Surface texture
- Fine-grain noise.
- Subtle spiral or turbulence near the face/head.
- Thin nervous lines running along arms/hands.
- Slight flickering around fingers.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| High HR | quick tight pulse in chest/neck |
| High EDA | dense head/chest noise |
| Low activity | aura stays close to body |
| SCR amplitude | sharp small ripples |
| Low motion + high arousal | visual tension without large external motion |

### Avoid
- Large explosive glitches
- Very bright energetic particles
- Relaxed wide aura
- Slow sleepy visuals

---

## 4. Active

### Visual feeling
Active should feel energetic, powerful, open, and outward-moving.

### Color palette
- Main color: bright cyan / electric blue
- Secondary color: white-blue highlights
- Optional accent: bright teal
- Brightness: high
- Contrast: medium-high

### Body appearance
- Body looks strong and illuminated.
- Muscle lines or energy channels can be visible.
- Hands, legs, shoulders, and chest are emphasized.
- Reflection is clear, not corrupted.

### Aura behavior
- Aura expands outward from the body.
- Aura is larger than in Calm.
- Aura responds strongly to movement.
- Edges can trail behind limbs.

### Motion style
- Particle trails follow hands and legs.
- Energy waves move outward from the torso.
- Motion is fast but controlled.
- Visuals should feel intentional, not chaotic.

### Surface texture
- Energy lines.
- Holographic muscle contours.
- Bright trails from moving limbs.
- Rhythmic glow around chest and shoulders.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| High ACC magnitude | stronger limb trails |
| High gyro variance | rotating energy field |
| High HR during movement | stronger body glow |
| Moderate/low EDA | clean energy instead of glitch |
| High activity level | more outward particles |

### Avoid
- Heavy red stress visuals
- Tight compressed aura
- Dull/low-energy body
- Random chaotic tearing

---

## 5. Fatigued

### Visual feeling
Fatigued should feel heavy, slow, dim, and low-energy.

### Color palette
- Main color: deep blue / desaturated cyan
- Secondary color: gray-blue
- Brightness: low
- Contrast: low to medium

### Body appearance
- Reflection looks dimmer and heavier.
- Body edges are soft and slightly blurred.
- Lower body can appear visually heavy.
- Torso and shoulders may look weighted down.

### Aura behavior
- Aura is weak and close to the body.
- Aura may drift downward like gravity is pulling it.
- Glow is uneven and fading.
- No sharp pulse or high-frequency vibration.

### Motion style
- Slow downward motion.
- Heavy blur trails.
- Delayed response to movement.
- Minimal particles.

### Surface texture
- Soft blur.
- Low opacity fog over the body.
- Slow-moving dark gradients.
- Weak glow around chest and limbs.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| Low HR / below baseline | weak slow pulse |
| Near-zero activity | low particle count |
| Temperature declining | colder/desaturated color |
| Low motion | downward aura drift |
| Low EDA + low motion | dull body clarity rather than glitch |

### Avoid
- Fast glitch
- Bright energetic aura
- High particle density
- Red stress colors unless fatigue is combined with stress

---

## 6. Overstimulated

### Visual feeling
Overstimulated should feel overloaded, chaotic, too bright, fragmented, and unstable.

### Color palette
- Main color: cyan + magenta collision
- Secondary color: red or white flashes
- Brightness: high but unstable
- Contrast: very high

### Body appearance
- Reflection looks fragmented.
- Face/head can distort or swirl.
- Body edges may split into multiple delayed silhouettes.
- The whole figure feels overloaded by too much signal.

### Aura behavior
- Aura is large but broken.
- Aura expands and tears apart.
- Multiple aura layers overlap and flicker.
- Shape is asymmetrical and unstable.

### Motion style
- Fast chaotic movement.
- Pixel tearing.
- Camera shake or body-edge shake.
- Particle bursts in multiple directions.
- Unstable feedback loops.

### Surface texture
- Heavy glitch.
- Digital tearing.
- High-frequency noise.
- Swirling distortion around head.
- Broken scanlines.

### Suggested visual mapping
| Data source | Visual parameter |
|---|---|
| Very high HR | rapid full-body pulse |
| Very high EDA | strong displacement/glitch |
| High gyro variance | unstable rotation / camera shake |
| High SCR amplitude | strong flash/burst |
| High SCR frequency | repeated glitch events |

### Avoid
- Calm smooth gradients
- Clean active energy look
- Low-contrast visuals
- Slow sleepy motion

# Unified Body Zone Mapping

Use the same body zones across all states so the system feels coherent.

| Body zone | Meaning | Visual use |
|---|---|---|
| Head | mental load / anxiety / overstimulation | noise, swirl, glitch, clarity |
| Chest | heart rate / breathing / activation | pulse, glow, expansion |
| Hands | tension / agency / interaction | flicker, trails, particle emission |
| Torso | overall energy state | brightness, texture density |
| Legs | activity / fatigue / grounding | trails, heaviness, stability |
| Outer aura | arousal level | size, turbulence, tightness |
| Ground plane | stability / grounding | grid stability, vibration, weight |

# Global Visual Parameters

These parameters can be controlled continuously by sensor-derived scores.

| Visual parameter | Data-driven control |
|---|---|
| Pulse speed | HR |
| Pulse irregularity | HR variance / SCR frequency |
| Glitch amount | EDA / stress score / overstimulation score |
| Aura size | activity score / calm score |
| Aura tightness | anxiety score |
| Blur amount | fatigue score |
| Particle density | activity score / overstimulation score |
| Camera shake | gyro variance / overstimulation score |
| Brightness | energy score |
| Color hue | current state |
| Reflection clarity | calm score |
| Surface noise | EDA / SCR frequency |

# Recommended State Shader System

Use one consistent holographic body base, then switch shader parameters by state.

```text
Base visual:
blue holographic human reflection
+ body silhouette
+ aura layer
+ surface texture layer
+ particle layer
+ ground grid
```

State modifiers:

```text
Calm:
smooth shader + soft cyan aura + low noise

Stressed:
red/orange pulse + chest/head glitch + tight aura

Anxious:
violet tight aura + head noise + micro-jitter

Active:
bright cyan energy trails + outward particles + strong glow

Fatigued:
low brightness + soft blur + downward aura drift

Overstimulated:
heavy glitch + broken aura + magenta/cyan tearing + camera shake
```

# Design Principle

The mirror should not only label a state. It should make the state visible on the body.

```text
Calm = clear reflection
Stressed = compressed noisy reflection
Anxious = tight vibrating aura
Active = outward energy trails
Fatigued = dim heavy body
Overstimulated = fragmented overloaded body
```
